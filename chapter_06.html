Characteristics of Air Traffic at IGI Airport

Examining the multidimensional patterns of network characteristics at IGI Airport, it is observed that there are certain peak traffic hours at IGI Airport that includes type of aircraft from slow climb aircraft to the operation of A380. Helicopter movements, VVIP movement, operation of Indian Air Force aircraft, non-schedule movement, charted flights, observation of Drone movement etc. also takes place from IGI Airport with the scheduled movement.

RELEASING FASTER DEPARTURE BEHIND SLOW MOVING DEPARTURE

Necessary coordination with approach control is to be affected by tower when a faster aircraft is to be released behind a slow moving departure. The coordination procedure in all such cases for strict compliance by tower controllers is as below:

  • No coordination will be required, if preceding aircraft is category C/D (except A340/ AN124) and succeeding departure is released after 2 minutes.
  • For other situations aerodrome controller (N/S/M) will first coordinate with approach departure before releasing the aircraft.

INCREASED SPACING FOR DEPARTURE OF AN124 DUE PERFORMANCE

Aircraft type AN124 has performance limitation and it takes 3-4 minutes to commence take-off roll after receiving take off clearance.

All tower controllers should coordinate for extra spacing between arrivals when such an aircraft type is planned for departure between arrivals

ACCOMMODATING ARC IL76 AIRCRAFT ON RUNWAY 29L/11R

The competent authority has approved that IL-76 type of aircraft of ARC shall be accommodated on Runway 29L/11R in case of unavailability of Runway 28/10.
