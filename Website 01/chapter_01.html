Chapter 1

Airspace Structure

ATS UNITS AND AREA OF JURISDICTION

DELHI AERODROME CONTROL

Aerodrome control shall be responsible for control of all traffic on maneuvering area. No airspace has been allocated to aerodrome control. However, aerodrome control shall be responsible for control of:

  • Arriving aircraft that have been released to it by approach control unit.
  • Departing aircraft until such aircraft are released, normally immediately after getting airborne, to approach control unit.

DELHI APPROACH CONTROL

Approach control unit shall be responsible for control of traffic within 60 NM around DPN in Delhi TMA at and below F140.

Corridor bounded by coordinates 2952N07551E; 2943N07555E; 2933N 07519E; 2942N 07518E, 2952N07551E from FL100 to FL120 is reserved for Indian Air Force aircraft crossing ATS route. This Airspace is not available for civil flights.
